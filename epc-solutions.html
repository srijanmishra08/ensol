Solar EPC Services

ENSOL provides comprehensive EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) services for solar power projects. With decades of experience in power infrastructure and in house manufacturing capabilities, we deliver high quality solar installations that maximize energy generation and return on investment.

01

Site Assessment

Comprehensive site survey including solar irradiance analysis, structural assessment, electrical infrastructure review, and feasibility study.

02

Engineering Design

Detailed engineering including structural design, electrical single line diagram, array layout optimization, and performance simulation.

03

Procurement

Quality procurement of solar modules, inverters, mounting structures (manufactured in house), cables, and electrical components from trusted suppliers.

04

Construction

Professional installation by trained teams with focus on quality, safety, and adherence to project timelines.

05

Commissioning

Complete testing, grid synchronization, DISCOM approvals, and system handover with performance documentation.

Complete BOS solutions for solar EPC contractors and project developers

Solar Balance of System

Balance of System (BOS) includes all components of a solar installation except the solar panels. ENSOL supplies comprehensive BOS packages including mounting structures, electrical components, cables, and protection devices. Our one stop BOS supply simplifies procurement for EPC contractors and ensures component compatibility.

BOS Components We Supply

Module Mounting Structures

Complete range of solar mounting systems for all project types:

  • Fixed tilt ground mount structures
  • Rooftop mounting systems (RCC/Metal sheet)
  • Carport and canopy structures
  • Solar pump mounting structures
  • Seasonal tilt adjustable systems
  • Agrivoltaics elevated structures
Manufactured In House

Solar Cables & Wiring

UV resistant, flame retardant cables for solar applications:

  • DC Solar cables (4mm² to 16mm²)
  • AC Power cables (LT/HT)
  • Earthing cables and conductors
  • Control and communication cables
  • Cable management accessories

ACDB / DCDB Panels

Pre assembled distribution boxes for solar systems:

  • DC Distribution Boards (String level)
  • AC Distribution Boards (Inverter output)
  • Main Junction Boxes (MJB)
  • String monitoring units
  • Lightning arresters and SPD

Connectors & Accessories

High quality interconnection components:

  • MC4 compatible connectors
  • Y Branch connectors
  • Fuse connectors
  • Cable glands and lugs
  • Module clamps and fasteners

Protection Devices

Safety and protection equipment for solar systems:

  • DC and AC surge protection
  • DC isolators and switches
  • Circuit breakers (MCB/MCCB)
  • Arc fault detection devices
  • Grounding equipment

Earthing & Lightning

Complete earthing and lightning protection systems:

  • Maintenance free earthing electrodes
  • Copper bonded rods
  • Lightning arresters
  • Earthing strips and conductors
  • Earth pit accessories
