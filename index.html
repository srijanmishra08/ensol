Who We Are

Simplifying Infrastructure Procurement

Since 1983, PLA has been engineering India's growth story

Power Line Accessories is a 40+ year old Indian manufacturing organization engaged in fabricated steel structures, electrical equipment, and heavy engineering solutions.

We've evolved from a complex switch assembly unit into a multi-vertical infrastructure manufacturing banner, offering a wide range of products under one roof. Our purpose is simple: remove friction from procurement by becoming a single, dependable manufacturing and supply hub.
