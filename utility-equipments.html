33 kV Isolators

33 kV isolators are essential switching devices used in medium voltage distribution systems. These isolators provide a visible break for safe maintenance and operation of electrical equipment. ENSOL manufactures high quality 33 kV isolators that meet stringent national standards and are CPRI tested for reliability.

Centre Break Type

Double arm rotation providing clear visible air gap

Double Break Type

Horizontal rotation with dual break contacts

CPRI Tested

Type tested at Central Power Research Institute

Earthing Facility

Optional integral earthing blade for maintenance safety

AB Switch (Air Break Switch)

AB Switches are pole mounted air break switches used for load switching and isolation in distribution networks. These switches provide a visible break and are commonly used on HT lines for load management and isolation during maintenance work.

Gang Operated

Three pole simultaneous operation for balanced switching

Arc Quenching

Horn gap design for effective arc extinguishing

Hot Line Operation

Designed for load making and breaking operations

Easy Installation

Ready to mount on standard poles and structures

Drop Out Fuse (DO Fuse)

Drop Out Fuses are pole mounted protection devices used in distribution networks to protect transformers and lateral lines from overcurrent and short circuit faults. When a fault occurs, the fuse element melts and the fuse carrier drops down, providing visual indication and isolation.

Fast Fault Clearing

Quick fuse operation to protect downstream equipment

Visual Indication

Dropped position indicates fault location

Coordination Capability

Time current characteristics for proper coordination

Weather Resistant

All weather outdoor operation capability
