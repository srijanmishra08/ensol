Who We Are

Simplifying Infrastructure Procurement

Since 1981, ENSOL has been engineering India's growth story

PLA Ensol Private Limited (formerly known as Power Line Accessories) is a 45+ year old organisation engaged in delivering solutions catered to Power, Solar and Infrastructure segments.

We've evolved from a complex switch assembly unit into a multi-vertical infrastructure manufacturing banner. With our state-of-the-art steel fabrication unit, we deliver precision-engineered components that form the backbone of growth for communities and India as a whole.

Our purpose is simple: remove friction from procurement by becoming a single, dependable manufacturing and supply hub for all your project needs.
