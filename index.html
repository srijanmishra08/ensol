Who We Are

Simplifying Infrastructure Procurement

Since 1983, PLA has been engineering India's growth story

Power Line Accessories is a 40+ year old Indian manufacturing organization engaged in fabricated steel structures, electrical equipment, and heavy engineering solutions.

We've evolved from a complex switch assembly unit into a multi-vertical infrastructure manufacturing banner. With our state-of-the-art steel fabrication unit, we deliver precision-engineered components that form the backbone of industrial growth.

Our purpose is simple: remove friction from procurement by becoming a single, dependable manufacturing and supply hub for all your infrastructure needs.
